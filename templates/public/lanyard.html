🎽 Get Your Free Lanyard

Once you've picked at least one tournament session, you can request your free lanyard.

Why a lanyard?

It's our visual signal that you're open to connecting with other CourtSide Club members in real life at events. It's simple, social, and helps break the ice.

How to get one:

  1. Log in and select at least one tournament session you're attending
  2. Go to your account and click "Order Lanyard"
  3. Enter your shipping details

That's it! Shipping is free, and it's a one-time thing — keep it and wear it proudly.
