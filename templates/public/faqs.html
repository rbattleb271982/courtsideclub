Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about CourtSide Club

Getting Started

CourtSide Club is an exclusive community for tennis fans who want to connect with like-minded enthusiasts at tournaments. We help you find and meet other passionate fans, making your tournament experience more social and memorable.

Simply click "Join Now" and create your account. Once you're verified, you'll have access to our tournament community features and can start connecting with other fans at upcoming events.

Tournaments & Events

We cover major ATP and WTA tournaments including Grand Slams, Masters 1000/WTA 1000 events, and select ATP 500/WTA 500 tournaments. We're constantly expanding our coverage based on member interest and attendance.

Lanyard Program

Our lanyard is a subtle way to identify fellow CourtSide Club members at tournaments. It features a QR code that other members can scan to connect with you, making it easier to start conversations and meet new people.

Yes, completely free including shipping. We want to remove any barriers to connecting with fellow tennis fans. Just verify your tournament attendance and we'll send you a lanyard at no cost.

Privacy & Safety

We only collect information necessary for connecting you with other fans. Your personal details are never shared without permission, and you control what information other members can see about you.

We verify all members and meetings happen in public tournament venues. However, as with any social interaction, use your best judgment. We provide guidelines for safe meetups and encourage meeting in groups when possible.
