Frequently Asked Questions

Do I have to pay for a lanyard?

Nope! It's totally free if you've signed up and selected at least one tournament session.

Can I see who else is attending a tournament?

Yes — once logged in, just visit any tournament page to see who's going and who's open to meeting up.

What's the point of the lanyard?

It's a visual signal that you're part of CourtSide Club and open to connecting with others at the event.

Can I invite a friend?

Yes! Once you sign up, you'll be able to refer friends and unlock perks.
