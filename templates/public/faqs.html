Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about connecting with fellow tennis fans at tournaments.

Getting Started

What is CourtSide Club? +

CourtSide Club is a community platform that helps tennis fans connect with each other at tournaments. We make it easy to find fellow fans who share your passion and are attending the same events.

How do I join CourtSide Club? +

Simply click "Sign Up" and create your account. Tell us which tournaments you're planning to attend, and we'll help you connect with other fans who'll be there too.

Is CourtSide Club free to use? +

Yes! CourtSide Club is completely free to join and use. We even provide free lanyards to help you connect with other members at tournaments.

How It Works

How do I find other fans at tournaments? +

After you RSVP to a tournament and select your sessions, you'll see other members who'll be attending the same matches. You can choose to connect with people and meet up at the event.

What's the lanyard system? +

When you RSVP to a tournament, you can order a free CourtSide Club lanyard. Wearing it at the tournament signals to other members that you're open to connecting. It's an easy, non-intrusive way to spot fellow community members.

Do I have to meet people in person? +

Not at all! You can participate in CourtSide Club as much or as little as you want. Some members just like seeing who else is attending, while others actively seek out meetups. It's entirely up to you.

Safety & Privacy

Is my personal information kept private? +

Yes, we take privacy seriously. We only share basic information like your first name and tournament attendance with other members. Your email, address, and other personal details remain private.

How do you ensure member safety? +

All meetups happen in public places (tennis tournaments) with lots of people around. We encourage members to stay in public areas and trust their instincts. You can always opt out of connecting with specific individuals.

Tournaments

Which tournaments does CourtSide Club cover? +

We cover major ATP and WTA tournaments including Grand Slams, Masters 1000/WTA 1000 events, and 500-level tournaments. Check our tournaments page for the full schedule.

What if I can't make it to a tournament I RSVP'd for? +

No problem! Just update your RSVP status in your profile. This helps other members get an accurate count of who'll actually be attending.
