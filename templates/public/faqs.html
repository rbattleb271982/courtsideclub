Frequently Asked Questions

🎟️ Signing Up & Getting Started
Do I have to pay for a lanyard?

Nope! It's totally free if you've signed up and selected at least one tournament session.

How do I sign up?

Just click "Join Now" on the homepage. You'll create a quick profile and select which tournaments and sessions you're attending.

What happens after I sign up?

You'll be able to see who else is going to tournaments, opt in to meetups, and order your free lanyard.

Can I invite a friend?

Yes! Once you sign up, you'll be able to refer friends and unlock perks.

🧑‍🤝‍🧑 Meeting Up with Members
Can I see who else is attending a tournament?

Yes — once logged in, just visit any tournament page to see who's going and who's open to meeting up.

What's the point of the lanyard?

It's a visual signal that you're part of CourtSide Club and open to connecting with others at the event.

How do I opt in to meet up?

On each tournament page, you can toggle whether you're open to meeting. You'll also choose which sessions you're attending.

Is there an official meet-up spot?

Some events will have suggested meeting spots or times — we'll include those in your reminder emails if available.

🧳 Attending Tournaments
Can I attend more than one tournament?

Absolutely — just add them to your profile and select which sessions you're going to.

What if my plans change?

You can update your attendance or session selections at any time from your dashboard.

Do I have to attend with friends?

Not at all. CourtSide Club is built for solo fans, friend groups, and everyone in between.

📦 Lanyard & Perks
When will my lanyard arrive?

Once you order it, we'll ship it to you in advance of your selected tournament(s). You'll get a tracking number too.

Do I get a new lanyard for each tournament?

No — it's a one-time lanyard you can use across multiple events.

Are there other perks?

Yes! We're working on exclusive offers and extras for active members — stay tuned.
