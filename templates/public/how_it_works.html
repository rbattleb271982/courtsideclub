How It Works

🎾 Step 1: Create your account

Use your email or social login to join. It's free and takes 1 minute.

📍 Step 2: Tell us which tournaments you're attending

Select which days and sessions you'll be there. You'll see who else is going.

🤝 Step 3: Meet people in real life

Look for the lanyard — our subtle signal that someone's part of CourtSide Club and open to meeting.

🎽 Step 4: Get your free lanyard

Once you've signed up for at least one session, you can request your lanyard — we'll ship it free.

💬 Step 5: Stay connected

We'll remind you who's going, suggest meet-up spots, and let you share photos post-event.
