Why CourtSide Club Was Started

We created CourtSide Club to make tennis more social, more welcoming, and more fun.

Join Login

Our Story

It started at Wimbledon 2023. I was sitting alone during a rain delay, watching hundreds of tennis fans around me doing the same thing — scrolling their phones, waiting for play to resume. Everyone seemed to share the same passion, but no one was talking.

That's when it hit me: we had created this incredible global community of tennis lovers, but we had no way to actually connect with each other when it mattered most — at the tournaments themselves.

CourtSide Club was born from that moment of realization. We wanted to bridge the gap between online tennis fandom and real-world connections, creating a space where the magic of shared passion could flourish naturally.

Our Vision

Members-Club Experience

Exclusive perks and experiences that make being a tennis fan feel special, from priority access to unique events and insider content.

Trip Planning Tools

Comprehensive tools for coordinating tournament trips, from group bookings to local recommendations from fellow fans.

Exclusive Benefits

As our community grows, unlock special benefits like group discounts, early access to tickets, and VIP experiences.

Real Connections

Focus on meaningful in-person connections at tournaments, not just another social media platform with endless scrolling.

Our Principles

Community-First

Every decision we make prioritizes the community's needs and experiences over profit.

Fan-Led

Privacy-Respecting

Your personal information stays personal. We believe in connections, not data collection.

Match-Day Magic
