Our Story

CourtSide Club was born from a simple observation: tennis fans are passionate, dedicated, and incredibly knowledgeable, but they often experience tournaments in isolation. Whether you're at Wimbledon, the US Open, or a local ATP event, you're surrounded by fellow fans, yet there's no easy way to connect.

We created CourtSide Club to change that. Our platform helps tennis enthusiasts find each other at tournaments, share their passion for the sport, and create lasting connections that extend beyond the final point.

Every feature we build—from tournament RSVPs to our signature lanyard system—is designed with one goal in mind: making tennis more social, more welcoming, and more fun for everyone involved.

What We Believe

1

Community First

Tennis is better when shared. We prioritize building genuine connections between fans who love the sport as much as you do.

2

Inclusive Access

Whether you're a casual fan or a tennis encyclopedia, there's a place for you in our community. All passion levels welcome.

3

Authentic Experiences

We focus on real connections at real tournaments. No algorithms, no artificial engagement—just tennis fans connecting naturally.
