About CourtSide Club

CourtSide Club connects tennis fans attending the same tournaments, creating a community of passionate enthusiasts.

Our Mission

To enhance the tournament experience by helping tennis fans connect with each other while attending major tournaments around the world.

How It Started

CourtSide Club was created by tennis fans for tennis fans. We noticed that while attending tournaments, there were countless opportunities to meet like-minded enthusiasts, but no easy way to connect before or during the event. Our platform was designed to solve this problem.

Join Our Community

By joining CourtSide Club, you'll become part of a global community of tennis fans who share your passion for the sport. Sign up today to start connecting with other fans at your next tournament.
