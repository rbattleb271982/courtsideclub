Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 15, 2025

Information We Collect

At CourtSide Club, we collect the following types of information:

  • Account information: name, email address, and password
  • Profile information: location and other optional information you provide
  • Tournament attendance: which tournaments you plan to attend
  • Shipping information: if you order a lanyard

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the CourtSide Club service
  • Help you connect with other tennis fans at tournaments
  • Send notifications about tournaments you're attending
  • Deliver lanyards to your shipping address
  • Improve and optimize our platform

Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited information with:

  • Other CourtSide Club users who are attending the same tournaments (only if you opt-in)
  • Service providers who help us operate our platform
  • Shipping partners to deliver lanyards

Your Choices

You can:

  • Update or delete your account information
  • Opt out of being visible to other users
  • Disable email notifications
  • Request deletion of your account

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: privacy@courtsideclub.app
